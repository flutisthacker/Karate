Welcome to a Goju Ryu karate

The word Goju-Ryu Kenwakai is derived from the Japanese term. Here, Go means hard, Jyu means soft, Ryu means art, Kenwakai means a group process for strong peace and Karate means self defense without any tools. Thus in one sentence the hard and soft art of self defense for peace. 10th Dan Hansi Akio Takahasi was the founder of Kenwakai Karate. Kenwakai Karate is also one of the Gojuryu Karate including in W.K.F Hansi Akio Takahasi had started his Karate journey from Gojuryu Karate and spend long time in this Karate and work hard. He participated in many national & international competition and perform many unforgettable performance and win many medals.
